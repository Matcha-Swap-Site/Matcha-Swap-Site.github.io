Introduction to Matcha Swap

Matcha Swap is a leading decentralized exchange (DEX) aggregator powered by the 0x protocol. Matcha allows users to discover the best prices for token swaps across multiple DEXs and liquidity sources in one seamless interface. As part of the broader DeFi ecosystem, Matcha is designed to offer efficient, transparent, and cost-effective crypto trading with a strong focus on user experience.

What is Matcha Swap?

Matcha Swap aggregates liquidity from a wide range of decentralized exchanges, such as Uniswap, SushiSwap, Curve, Balancer, and more. This means that when you swap tokens on Matcha, the platform automatically searches for the best prices and lowest slippage routes, executing your trade at the most favorable rate. Matcha also supports limit orders, cross-chain swaps, and a non-custodial approach for maximum security.

  • Aggregates liquidity from 30+ DEXs
  • Intuitive and easy-to-use UI
  • Supports major blockchains: Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Optimism, Arbitrum, Base
  • Private swaps with no gas or routing surprises
  • Fully non-custodial: you retain control of your assets

  2. Select Tokens: Choose the token you want to swap and the one you want to receive.
  3. Check Best Prices: Matcha will display the best available prices aggregated from all connected DEXs.

  5. Confirm and Execute: Approve the transaction in your wallet and wait for blockchain confirmation.
  6. Track Transaction: View real-time transaction status and history in the Matcha dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – Matcha Swap

Is Matcha Swap Safe?

Yes, Matcha Swap is built on audited 0x protocol smart contracts and is fully non-custodial. Your assets always remain in your control.

What Networks Does Matcha Support?

Matcha supports Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, Optimism, Arbitrum, and Base for seamless cross-chain trading.

Are There Any Fees?

Matcha charges a small service fee, always transparently displayed before swap execution. Standard network gas fees also apply.

Does Matcha Support Limit Orders?

Yes. You can place limit orders and Matcha will execute the trade once your price target is met.

Can I Use Hardware Wallets?

Yes, hardware wallets like Ledger and Trezor can be connected for added security.

Where Can I See My Trade History?

The Matcha dashboard allows users to view their transaction history and monitor active and past trades.
